<p><strong>The Resin Dental Cements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Adhesive Resin Dental Cements</li><li>Universal Adhesive Resin Dental Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin dental cements market is primarily categorized into two types: self-adhesive resin dental cements and universal adhesive resin dental cements. Self-adhesive resin cements bond to both enamel and dentin without the need for additional bonding agents, streamlining the cementation process. Universal adhesive resin cements, on the other hand, provide versatility, allowing for use with a variety of bonding protocols and materials, making them suitable for multiple clinical situations, including direct and indirect restorations.</p></p>
