<p><p>The head-mounted snorkeling mask market primarily consists of two types: half face masks and full face masks. Half face masks cover only the eyes and nose, allowing for easy breathing through a snorkel, making them suitable for experienced snorkelers familiar with the technique. Full face masks, on the other hand, cover the entire face, providing a wider field of vision and enabling natural breathing through both the nose and mouth. This makes full face masks more user-friendly, especially for beginners and casual snorkelers.</p></p>
