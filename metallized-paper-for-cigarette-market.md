<p><p>Metallized paper for the cigarette market primarily consists of two types: lamination type and vacuum metallization type. Lamination type involves adhering a thin layer of metal to paper, enhancing its barrier properties against moisture and light, thus preserving tobacco quality. In contrast, vacuum metallization type uses a vacuum process to deposit a metal layer, providing a more uniform and thinner coating, which is effective in reducing weight and increasing visual appeal. Both types enhance branding and functionality in cigarette packaging.</p></p>
