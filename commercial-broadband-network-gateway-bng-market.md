<p><p>The Commercial Broadband Network Gateway (BNG) market encompasses various types, including Traditional BNG and virtual BNG (vBNG). Traditional BNG relies on hardware-based solutions that provide functionalities such as subscriber management and bandwidth allocation within fixed networks. In contrast, vBNG utilizes virtualization technologies to offer similar services in a more flexible and scalable manner, reducing costs and enhancing operational efficiency. Both types are essential for managing broadband connections and delivering high-quality internet services to end-users.</p></p>
