<p><ul><li>Expanded Polypropylene Foams</li><li>Extruded Polypropylene Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene foams market primarily comprises two types: expanded polypropylene (EPP) foams and extruded polypropylene (XPP) foams. Expanded polypropylene foams are produced through a process that incorporates gas into the material, resulting in lightweight, durable, and versatile foam suitable for packaging, automotive, and insulation applications. Extruded polypropylene foams, on the other hand, are created through a continuous process that offers a denser structure, making them ideal for applications requiring more rigidity and strength, such as construction and industrial uses.</p></p>
