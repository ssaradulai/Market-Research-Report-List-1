<p><p>The SMT Repair and Inspection Equipment market encompasses various types of technologies essential for ensuring quality in electronics manufacturing. Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems use cameras to detect defects on circuit boards by comparing images with known standards. Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) systems assess the accuracy of solder paste application before component placement. Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) employs X-ray technology to examine hidden solder joints and connections in assembled packages, ensuring reliability and performance in final products. Together, they enhance quality control and reduce production defects.</p></p>
