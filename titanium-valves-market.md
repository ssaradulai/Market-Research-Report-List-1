<p><p>The titanium valves market encompasses several types, each serving specific functions in fluid control systems. Ball valves provide quick shut-off capabilities with low pressure drops and high reliability. Globe valves are designed for throttling flow, offering precise control. Butterfly valves are lightweight and compact, suitable for large-volume flow regulation. Other types of valves may include check valves and gate valves, each designed for particular applications in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and water management, emphasizing durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
