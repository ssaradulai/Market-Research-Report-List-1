<p><p>Pulmonary drug delivery systems encompass various devices for administering medications directly to the lungs. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist, making it easy for patients to inhale, particularly beneficial for those with respiratory challenges. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) dispense a powdered form of medication, requiring patients to inhale deeply to activate the delivery. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) release a specific dose of aerosolized medication with each actuation, offering a portable and convenient option for asthma and COPD management.</p></p>
