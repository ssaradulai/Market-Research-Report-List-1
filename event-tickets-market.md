<p><p>The event ticket market consists of two primary types: paper tickets and electronic tickets. Paper tickets are traditional physical tickets often printed on cardstock, providing a tangible collectible experience but are susceptible to loss and fading. In contrast, electronic tickets, or e-tickets, are digital formats sent via email or mobile apps, offering convenience and instant access. They streamline entry processes and reduce costs associated with printing. Both types serve to facilitate attendance at events while catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
