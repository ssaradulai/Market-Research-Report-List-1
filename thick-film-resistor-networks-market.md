<p><strong>The Thick Film Resistor Networks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-In-Line</li><li>Dual-In-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick film resistor networks are essential components used in various electronic circuits, available in multiple configurations. Single-In-Line (SIL) resistor networks feature components arranged in a single row, making them compact and suitable for applications with limited space. Dual-In-Line (DIL) resistor networks, on the other hand, have components arranged in two parallel rows, allowing for easier mounting on circuit boards and facilitating more versatile connections. Both types are widely used for their reliability and efficiency in managing resistance levels in circuits.</p></p>
