<p><p>The external mold releases market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and solvent-free external mold releases. Solvent-based external mold releases typically contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that help in easy application and removal but may raise environmental concerns. Conversely, solvent-free external mold releases are designed to be more environmentally friendly, offering minimal VOC emissions while still providing effective release properties. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods, ensuring efficient production processes.</p></p>
