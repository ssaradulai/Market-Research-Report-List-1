<p><p>Stretch sleeve labels are a type of packaging label made from flexible materials that fit snugly around the contours of a product, often used for bottles, cans, and other cylindrical items. These labels are popular for their high-quality graphics and ability to cover the entire surface of a container, enhancing product visibility and aesthetic appeal.</p><p>The Stretch Sleeve Labels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period.
