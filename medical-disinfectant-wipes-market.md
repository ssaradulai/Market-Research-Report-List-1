<p><p>The medical disinfectant wipes market is segmented into two main types: alcohol-free disinfectant wipes and alcohol disinfectant wipes. Alcohol-based disinfectant wipes contain high concentrations of isopropyl or ethyl alcohol, effectively killing a wide range of pathogens but may dry surfaces quickly. In contrast, alcohol-free disinfectant wipes utilize alternative antimicrobial agents, providing a gentler option suitable for sensitive surfaces and skin. Each type serves distinct needs in healthcare settings, balancing efficacy and surface compatibility for optimal infection control.</p></p>
