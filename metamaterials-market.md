<p><p>The metamaterials market encompasses various types that manipulate waves to achieve unique properties. Electromagnetic metamaterials manipulate electromagnetic waves, enabling applications like superlenses and cloaking. Optical metamaterials focus on light manipulation for advanced imaging and sensing technologies. Acoustic metamaterials control sound waves, leading to innovative noise reduction and sound manipulation techniques. Additionally, mechanical metamaterials possess unique structural properties, allowing engineers to create materials with customizable mechanical responses. Together, these categories showcase the diverse applications and potential of metamaterials across industries.</p></p>
