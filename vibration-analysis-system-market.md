<p><p>The vibration analysis system market is divided into two main types: online and offline systems. Online vibration analysis systems provide real-time monitoring of machinery conditions using permanent sensors, allowing for immediate detection of anomalies and predictive maintenance. In contrast, offline vibration analysis systems involve the periodic manual measurement of vibrations using portable equipment, which is then analyzed for maintenance planning. Both types are essential for ensuring equipment reliability and minimizing downtime in various industrial applications.</p></p>
