<p><p>The Single-Lead ECG Monitors market is divided into two main types: Recording-Based Single-Lead ECG Monitors and Real-Time Single-Lead ECG Monitors. Recording-Based monitors capture and store ECG data for later analysis, making them useful for diagnostic purposes and long-term monitoring. In contrast, Real-Time Single-Lead ECG Monitors provide immediate feedback, allowing for real-time assessment and monitoring of cardiac activity. Both types are essential in telemedicine, patient monitoring, and wearable health technology, catering to different clinical needs and settings.</p></p>
