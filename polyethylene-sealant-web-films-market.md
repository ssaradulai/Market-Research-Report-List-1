<p><p>Polyethylene sealant web films are categorized primarily into two types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) sealant web films. LDPE sealant films are known for their flexibility, moisture resistance, and clarity, making them suitable for packaging applications. In contrast, LLDPE sealant films offer enhanced strength, puncture resistance, and stretchability, which are advantageous in high-performance scenarios. Both types are widely utilized in food packaging, medical applications, and industrial uses, providing reliable sealing solutions in various environments.</p></p>
