<p><p>Pillow packing machines are essential in the packaging industry, and they come in two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal pillow packing machines are designed for products that lay flat, efficiently packaging items such as snacks or cosmetics in a side-sealed format. In contrast, vertical pillow packing machines are suited for bulk or loose products, such as granular foods or powders, utilizing gravity to fill and seal packages from the top down, ensuring efficiency and minimal waste.</p></p>
