<p><p>The adhesives used for radar and ultrasonic sensors are categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Solid adhesives, often in the form of pre-made sheets or blocks, offer durability and structural integrity in demanding environments. Paste adhesives, with a thicker consistency, allow for easy application and precision in positioning, making them suitable for various sensor mounting needs. Together, these adhesives enhance sensor performance and reliability.</p></p>
