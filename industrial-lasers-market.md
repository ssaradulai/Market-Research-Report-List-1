Solid-state lasers, often employing crystals or glass as the gain medium, are favored for high precision and versatile applications, including welding and material processing. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers, offering high beam quality and efficiency, making them ideal for metal cutting and marking. Each type has unique advantages tailored to different industrial needs.</p></p>
