<p><p>Fiber optic rotary joints (FORJs) are utilized to transmit optical signals while allowing for rotational movement. The market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel FORJs facilitate the transfer of light through one fiber, ideal for simpler applications requiring minimal data transmission. Multi-channel FORJs, on the other hand, support multiple fibers and enable the simultaneous transfer of various signals, making them suitable for complex systems such as military equipment, aerospace, and telecommunications where higher bandwidth is essential.</p></p>
