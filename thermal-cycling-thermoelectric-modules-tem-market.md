<p><p>The Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Modules (TEM) market is primarily divided into two types: Single Stage Thermal Cycling TEM and Multistage Thermal Cycling TEM. Single Stage TEMs utilize one thermoelectric element to transfer heat, suitable for applications with moderate temperature differentials. In contrast, Multistage TEMs employ multiple thermoelectric elements stacked together, allowing for greater temperature variations and efficiency, making them ideal for demanding cooling or heating requirements in various industries like electronics and automotive.</p></p>
