<p><p>The pressure oven market comprises primarily two types: pressure defoaming and vacuum pressure defoaming. Pressure defoaming utilizes increased atmospheric pressure to remove trapped air bubbles from materials, enhancing their quality and durability, commonly used in manufacturing composites and coatings. Vacuum pressure defoaming, on the other hand, involves reducing the pressure within the chamber to draw out air and gas, often used in resin infusion processes. Both methods improve product integrity and performance by ensuring better material compaction and adhesion.</p></p>
