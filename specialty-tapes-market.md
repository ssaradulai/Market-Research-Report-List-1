<p><p>The specialty tapes market is divided into woven and non-woven tape categories. Woven tapes are made from interlaced fibers, providing strength and durability, commonly used in construction, packaging, and automotive applications. Non-woven tapes, on the other hand, are produced from bonded fibers, offering flexibility and softness, making them ideal for medical, hygiene, and insulation applications. Both types serve distinct functions, catering to various industries where specific adhesion and performance characteristics are essential for optimal results.</p></p>
