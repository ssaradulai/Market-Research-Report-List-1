<p><p>The switch diode market comprises three primary types: General Switch Diodes, High-Speed Switching Diodes, and Ultra High-Speed Switching Diodes. General Switch Diodes are used for basic switching applications, handling moderate speeds and currents. High-Speed Switching Diodes facilitate faster switching, making them suitable for high-frequency applications like telecommunications.
