<p><p>The AI sweeping robot market is primarily categorized into two types: infrared sensor and ultrasonic bionic types. Infrared sensor robots utilize infrared technology to detect obstacles and navigate efficiently, making them ideal for homes and offices. In contrast, ultrasonic bionic robots leverage sound waves to create a spatial awareness of their surroundings, allowing for more complex maneuvering and obstacle avoidance. Both types enhance cleaning performance, but differ in their sensing methods and operational capabilities, catering to varied consumer needs.</p></p>
