<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Immunotherapy</li><li>Sublingual Immunotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Allergy immunotherapy market includes two main types: subcutaneous immunotherapy (SCIT) and sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT). SCIT involves injecting allergen extracts beneath the skin, promoting long-term tolerance by gradually desensitizing patients to specific allergens. This method requires regular clinic visits. In contrast, SLIT uses allergen tablets or drops placed under the tongue, offering a more convenient and at-home treatment option with a favorable safety profile. Both methods aim to reduce allergy symptoms and improve patients' quality of life.</p></p>
