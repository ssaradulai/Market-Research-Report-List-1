<p><strong>The Latte Instant Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Latte Instant Coffee market can be segmented based on the drying methods used in production: spray-drying and freeze-drying. Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating water from a liquid coffee extract, resulting in a fine powder that retains flavor and aroma while being cost-effective. In contrast, freeze-drying preserves more of the coffee's original qualities by freezing the extract and then removing moisture under vacuum, yielding a higher-quality product but often at a higher price. Each method caters to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
