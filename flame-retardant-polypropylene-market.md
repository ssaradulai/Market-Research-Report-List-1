<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polypropylene market comprises two primary types: Halogen Flame Retardant PP and Halogen Free Flame Retardant PP. Halogen Flame Retardant PP utilizes halogen compounds to impede combustion, offering effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. Conversely, Halogen Free Flame Retardant PP employs alternative fire retardants that minimize toxicity and environmental impact, becoming increasingly popular due to stricter regulations and rising demand for safer materials. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
