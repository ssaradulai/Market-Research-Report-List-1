<p><p>The female urinary incontinence products market is segmented into three main types: stress incontinence, full urinary incontinence, and true incontinence. Stress incontinence involves leakage during physical activities, often addressed with absorbent pads and specialized underwear. Full urinary incontinence refers to a complete loss of bladder control, requiring more absorbent products like adult diapers. True incontinence describes a condition with no control over urination at all, necessitating advanced solutions such as catheters or specialized containment products for effective management.</p></p>
