<p><p>Recruitment CRMs can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>Cloud-based recruitment CRMs are hosted on the vendor's servers, offering accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection, automatic updates, and scalability. They typically operate on a subscription model. </p><p>On-premises recruitment CRMs are installed and maintained locally on an organization’s servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
