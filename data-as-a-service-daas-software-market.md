<p><p>Data as a Service (DaaS) encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based DaaS delivers data through internet-based platforms, offering scalability, flexibility, and lower upfront costs, appealing to businesses that prefer minimal infrastructure investment. Conversely, on-premise DaaS involves deploying data solutions on local servers, providing enhanced security and control, which is essential for organizations with strict compliance requirements. Both models cater to different operational needs and preferences in data accessibility and management.</p></p>
