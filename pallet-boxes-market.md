<p><p>The pallet boxes market can be categorized into three main types: block pallets, stringer pallets, and customized pallets. Block pallets are constructed with wood or plastic blocks, providing superior stability and weight capacity, suitable for heavy loads. Stringer pallets utilize a series of stringers, offering a more economical option, ideal for lighter loads and easy handling. Customized pallets cater to specific industry needs, designed for unique dimensions or materials, ensuring optimal efficiency and protection for various products during storage and transport.</p></p>
