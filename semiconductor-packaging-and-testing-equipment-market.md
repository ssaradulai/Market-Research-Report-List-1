<p><p>The semiconductor packaging and testing equipment market includes various types essential for the production and quality assurance of semiconductor devices. Probers analyze wafer test sites for electrical characteristics, while bonders connect semiconductor chips to packaging substrates using specialized techniques. Dicing machines cut wafers into individual chips, facilitating further processing. Sorters categorize packaged chips based on performance, and handlers automate the transfer of semiconductor devices during testing and packaging. Additional equipment encompasses other technologies that support these functions in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
