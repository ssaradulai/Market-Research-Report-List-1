<p><p>The cast and ductile iron valve market includes various types, such as butterfly, gate, globe, ball, and check valves. Butterfly valves are used for regulating flow with a disc that pivots. Gate valves provide on/off control with a rising or non-rising stem mechanism. Globe valves, designed for throttling, feature a spherical body for flow control. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with a spherical disc, while check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional flow in pipelines. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
